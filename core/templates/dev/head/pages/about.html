Our Mission

To help anyone learn anything they want in an effective and enjoyable way.

Meet Maestro

Freehold Township Schools presents, Maestro, our online learning platform connecting and helping curious minds learn and teach. Maestro is full of interactive activities called 'explorations!'

Learn

Maestro recreates the experience of one-on-one tutoring. It adapts to your needs and lets you learn in the best way possible — by doing.

Teach

Maestro enables you to share your knowledge with people worldwide. Anyone can create and contribute to an exploration. Find out more in our participation playbook.
